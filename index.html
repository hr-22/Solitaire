How to Play

Goal

Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

Tableau rules

  • Cards must alternate red / black in colour.
  • Each card must be one rank lower than the card it goes on.
  • Only a King can start an empty column.
  • Click a face-down card to flip it.

Foundation rules

  • Start each foundation with an Ace of that suit.
  • Build up in the same suit: A → 2 → … → K.
  • Double-click any eligible card to send it automatically.

Drawing cards

  • Click the stock pile (top-left) to flip one card to the waste.
  • When the stock is empty, clicking it resets the waste back to stock (−100 pts).

Scoring

  • Waste → Tableau: +5
  • Any card → Foundation: +10
  • Foundation → Tableau: −15
  • Flip a card: +5
  • Recycling stock: −100
